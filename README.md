# Master Thesis – The Sectoral CCyB in Switzerland

## Description
Replication files for my Master Thesis "The Sectoral CCyB in Switzerland: 
Evidence from the COVID-19 Crisis and Potential Enhancements".

Natalia Jacob  
University of Zurich, Department of Economics

## Structure
- `data/` – Raw and processed data
- `scripts/` – R scripts and AI prompts for analysis
- `output/` – Tables and figures
